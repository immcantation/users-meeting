Immcantation Users Group Meeting 2020

Join us for the Immcantation Users Group Meeting 2020 to learn how researchers are applying the Immcantation Framework for high-throughput bulk and single-cell B cell receptor repertoire analysis. Submit an abstract to present your own research, or simply join us to learn how others are using Immcantation to advance knowledge of adaptive immunity.

This virtual users meeting will take place via videoconference on January 29th, 2020, from 12:00PM to 3:00PM (EST). The program consists of seven presentations chosen from submitted abstracts. See Program for details.

Registration is free . See Registration for details.
